Key Findings & Insights
Regional Health Disparities
Significant differences in health outcomes across geographic regions, especially between Sub-Saharan Africa, South Asia, and developed countries.
Temporal Trends
Steady global improvement in health indicators, with rapid gains in maternal and child health in developing regions over the last decade.
Environmental Correlations
Strong associations found between environmental factors (air, water, access) and population health, with urban-rural contrasts.
Socioeconomic Impact
GDP per capita and education were identified as key predictors of positive health outcomes, with threshold effects observed.
