Global Health Insights

by Trizzia Ellaine

Project Introduction

Welcome! This project explores global health trends by analyzing a variety of datasets related to life expectancy, access to healthcare, and disease burden. My goal is to transform complex data into meaningful insights that can inspire positive change and inform future strategies.

Project Goals

  • Visualize and compare health trends across regions using data analytics
  • Identify key drivers of health inequalities around the world
  • Offer actionable, data-driven recommendations for stakeholders

Data Sources & Methods

The data used for this analysis was sourced from the World Health Organization, World Bank, and Our World in Data.

I applied Python (with pandas and matplotlib) and Excel to clean, analyze, and visualize the data. My analysis included time series trends, correlation studies, and mapping key indicators.

Key Insights

  • Global life expectancy has generally improved, but significant disparities remain between high- and low-income countries.
  • Regions with limited access to essential healthcare continue to face challenges in reducing mortality rates.
  • Investment in health systems strongly correlates with better overall health outcomes.
